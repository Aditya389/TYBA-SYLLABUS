Course Objectives

There has been a paradigm shift in the structure of the Indian industrial sector and the policies governing it ever since the new era of globalization and liberalization has ushered in. This paper intends to equip the students with the knowledge about the fundamentals of Industrial Economics and also the latest policies relating to the Indian industry.

Course Outcomes

  • Learners will study the different contemporary issues of industrial sector.
  • Learners will know the problems of industries.
  • Learners will get the idea about productivity.
  • Learners will get with new policies and its impact on industries.
