Course Objectives

This course is designed to provide a sound understanding of advanced microeconomic theory. Since students are already familiar with perfect competition, this course focuses on the core areas of imperfect competition, welfare economics, and information economics.

Course Outcomes

  • Enables students to understand new market structures and imperfect competition.
  • Provides a strong grasp of welfare economics and the economics of information.
