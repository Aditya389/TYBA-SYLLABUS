Course Objectives

This paper introduces the concepts, theories, processes and policies regarding growth and development. The meaning of development as it has evolved over the years is clarified. The contemporary as well as classical theories of growth, development, and underdevelopment are considered in detail. Theories and issues related to population, poverty, inequality and human capital are included. Both urban and rural aspects of development are studied. The importance of technology, infrastructure, and planning in the development process is highlighted.

Course Outcomes

  • Enable students to apply and analyse issues in the development process.
  • Students will be able to identify issues related to Growth and Development.
  • Students will understand policy options and analyse measures taken for economic development.
